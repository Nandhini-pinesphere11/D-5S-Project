What 5S Means for Us

Our List of Features

SORT (SEIRI)

Everything from the workspace should be removed and sorted into three groups or bins: keep, remove, and holding area.

SET IN ORDER (SEITON)

Organize and arrange the appropriate items so that they are easy to access, use and return to their proper place.

SHINE (SEISOU)

The Shine step elevates the work area by ensuring thoroughly cleaned and inspected tools and other items.

STANDARDIZE (SEIKETSU)

Standardize, the fourth of the 5S's, involves putting the systems in place to ensure that everyone does things the same way.

SUSTAIN (SHITSUKE)

The Sustain step ensures that 5S is applied on an ongoing basis. It transforms your standardized 5S processes into regularly completed tasks.
